Kitchen Design and Fittings

From concept to completion, I will manage all aspects of your dream kitchen installation. I have over 30 years experience fitting kitchens and am fully qualified, accredited and will work with you to bring your kitchen ideas to life.

I have fitted kitchens for major retailers including MFI, B&Q, Homebase and Wren.

Registered with the British Institute of Kitchen Bedroom and Bathroom Installers (BIKBBI) which requires I be fully accredited and insured. Reg no N282396.

Holding Trade Accounts with various retailers means I can offer a competitive and clear pricing structure.
